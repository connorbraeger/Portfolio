About Me

Hello, my name is Connor Braeger and I have been a junior software engineer at Remodel Health in Indianapolis since May of 2021. My education background includes both graduating from Indiana University in 2017 with a B.S. in Chemistry and completion of the Eleven Fifty Academy Software Development program. This program was a 24-week immersive learning program for Software Development taught with industry-guided curriculum, real-world project-based learning, and 500+ hours of logged coding time and training.

Before starting my career as a software engineer, I worked primarily in analytical laboratories as a quality control chemist. I have performed chemical analyses in a variety of scientific indsutries including pharmaceuticals, oils, and bulk reagents. Having worked for years in the scientific field, I have developed an analytical framework for my critical thinking skills conducive to the problem solving required for software development.

Please feel free to check out my Github page or add me on LinkedIn if you want to learn more about me or some of my my projects!
